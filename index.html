About Me

System Wrangler

Hi! My name is John Russell. I am an Army Veteran with a diverse background in computing and communication technologies. I have worked in various government agencies including US Special Operations Command (USSOCOM), Space and Naval Warfare Systems Command (SPAWAR), Federal Bureau of Investigations (FBI) and National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency (NGA). With 15 years experience as a technologist & developer, I have acquired the skills and knowledge necessary to make your project a success. I created POC | Digital to expand on providing various professional services to commercial and government clients. POC| Digital provides unique and effective technical solutions, as well as an ability to rapidly assemble and effectively manage skilled teams of practitioners. Our primary service areas include: Cloud Management, Cyber Security, Data Management, DEVOPS and software development. I enjoy every step of the design process, from discussion and collaboration to concept and execution, but I find the most satisfaction in seeing the finished product do everything for you that it was created to do.
